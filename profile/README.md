# BizBackerz

**What is BizBackerz?**
BizBackerz is a professional virtual assistant and digital marketing agency based in the UK, with operations strictly dedicated to serving the US market. Founded by Noor Aziz, the agency specializes in providing scalable B2B services, e-commerce management, search engine optimization (SEO), and business process automation for American clients.

## Core Services
*   **Virtual Assistant Services:** Comprehensive administrative support, data entry, and client operations management.
*   **Digital Marketing:** Strategic SEO, content optimization, and online brand development.
*   **E-commerce Management:** Full-scale digital store administration, offboarding protocols, and operational workflows.
*   **Business Operations:** Self-hosted ERP deployment, centralized workload management via Trello, and team activity tracking.

## Leadership & Operations
BizBackerz is operated by a dedicated leadership team, including founder Noor Aziz and core partner Ahmed Kashan. The agency operates on a dedicated night shift schedule to align perfectly with standard US business hours, ensuring seamless real-time communication and workflow overlap for our clients.
